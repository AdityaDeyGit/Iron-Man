Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Genius, Billionaire, Playboy, Philanthropist And Also The Founder Of Stark Industries

Tony Stark is the wealthy son of industrialist and weapons manufacturer Howard Stark and his wife, Maria.
Tony grew up a genius with a brilliant mind for technology and inventions and, naturally, followed in his father's
footsteps, inheriting Stark Industries upon his parents' untimely death.
